Data Visualization Project & EDA Process

Apply knowledge of Excel and Tableau to complete a real-world data visualization project in teams, using the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) framework.

EDA is the process of examining and summarizing a dataset to understand its main characteristics, patterns, and relationships before more advanced analysis. Steps include:

  1. Ask questions
  2. Collect and import data
  3. Clean data
  4. Explore data
  5. Visualize data
  6. Analyze and interpret
  7. Share findings

Cover Letter

Structure
  1. Salutation: Professional greeting
  2. Intro: State role, objectives, source of job posting, and interests
  3. Body: 1–3 paragraphs referencing (not copying) your resume
  4. Making the Case: Provide evidence and examples of fit
  5. Closing: Thank reader, suggest next steps, request interview

Email Writing

Structure
  1. Subject line: Concise and specific
  2. Salutation: Correct spelling of name
  3. Body: Key point in first paragraph
  4. Signature: Relevant contact info (name, number, links)
  5. Spelling/Grammar: Double-check names, links, etc.

Example Intro: Dear [name], I hope your day is going well. I am [name] and I'm writing to/because X. [Explain how you got their contact.]
