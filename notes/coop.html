Data Visualization Project & EDA process

Apply Knowledge of Excel and Tableau to complete a real-world data visualization project in teams using the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) framework.
EDA is the process of examining and summarizing a dataset to better understand its main characteristics, patterns, and relationships between variables. It helps identify potential problems, errors, or interesting trends in the data before diving into more advanced analysis. The steps are:
1. Ask questions.
2. Collect and import data.
3. Clean data.
4. Explore data.
5. Visualize data.
6. Analyze and interpret.
7. Share findings.

Cover Letter

Structure
1. Salutation: Professional greeting.
2. Intro: Capture interest by naming the role you are applying for, your career objectives, how you heard about the job, and what interests you.
3. Body: 1-3 paragraphs referencing (not copying) your resume, showing you understand the need of the role and how you qualify.
4. Making the case: Provide evidence to back up your claims; include specifically where your experience can contribute to the organization.
5. Closing: Thank the reader and suggest next steps for you or them - this may be a request for an interview and/or a statement of your intent to follow up.

Email Writing

Structure
1. Subject line: Concise and specific.
2. Salutation: Appropriate "Hello" with name spelled correctly.
3. Structure: Key point in first paragraph.
4. Signature: Leave relevant contact info (name, number, relevant links).
5. Spelling/grammar: Double check names, links, etc.

Example Intro: "Dear [name], I hope your day is going well. I am [name] and I'm writing to/because X." Explain how you got their contact.
