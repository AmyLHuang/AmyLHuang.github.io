Definitions

  • Statistics: The study of the collection, analysis, and interpretation of data
  • Descriptive statistics: Describe or summarize the main features of a dataset
  • Inferential statistics: Make inferences about a dataset based on a sample of the data
  • Population: Every possible element that you are interested in measureing
  • Sample: A subset of a population
  • A/B testing: A way to compare two versions of something to find out which version performs better
