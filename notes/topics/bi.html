Business Intelligence

Business Intelligence (BI) refers to the systematic use of data in an organization. It encompasses the tools, technologies, and practices used to collect, integrate, analyze, and present business data for critical business operations. By leveraging BI, organizations can uncover insights, patterns, and trends, allowing them to make informed and strategic business decisions (aka data driven decision making).

Tableau

Tableau is an interactive visualization software tool owned by Salesforce, that allows users to explore and understand their data to influence business decisions. Tableau Desktop is a paid product but Tableau Public is free. All reports in Tableau Public are public once they're published.
