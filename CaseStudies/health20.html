Health2O

Mobile Application | Health & Fitness Tracking

View on GitHub

Introduction

Health2O is a mobile application designed to help users build healthier fitness habits by tracking three essential wellness areas: sleep, exercise, and hydration. With a simple and intuitive design, Health2O encourages users to maintain a balanced lifestyle through daily tracking and goal setting.

Goal

Health2O focuses on just three vital health areas — sleep, exercise, and hydration — and provides users with easy-to-use tracking tools, personalized reminders, and visual progress indicators to build sustainable habits over time.

Key Features

  • Daily tracking of sleep hours, exercise sessions, and water intake
  • Visual progress charts and habit streaks
  • Custom goal setting for each health area
  • Personalized reminders and notifications
  • Minimalist, user-friendly interface design

Technologies Used

React Native, Expo, JavaScript, CSS Modules
